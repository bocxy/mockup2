ABOUT BOCXY



Bocxy's vision and ideation has started 3 years ago in 2017, with the passion to make an impact in Salon and SPA industry across the globe. Bocxy technologies private limited was launched in mid 2020, and its the worlds 1st unified service and product platform for this industry. Our platform helps customers to book appointments and buy products in real time from millions of Salons and SPAs, not only on-premises, as well as homes services.



Our company's version is to make millions of Salon and SPA to go digital in seconds, win net new customers like never before and manage their existing customers relationships seamlessly through digital form. Bocxy's secret sauce is in 3 layers - "Continues Creativity(CC)", "Continues Innovation (CI)" and "Continues Learnings (CL)".
